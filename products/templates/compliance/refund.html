HOW CAN I MAKE A RETURN?

We accept returns on un-used product(s) sent back in the original condition with the original packaging. We allow up to 30 days from the delivery date. Please contact customer services to receive the return address. You are responsible for the cost of your returns.

Returns are typically processed within 1-2 working days of receiving the product. If you have any questions please contact us through our email or contact page.

We will notify you once we have refunded your payment. Payment will be made to the original method of payment. Please note this may take up to 10 working days depending on your provider.

This does not affect your statutory rights.
