Privacy & Cookies: Our promises

We will always keep your data safe and secure. So you are clued up, here’s why we need it and how we use it.

1. Only the good stuff

We will only use your data to process your order and deliver your products to you, improve our Poster Palace services, give you the most out of your Poster Palace experience, and keep you in the loop about things that might interest you.

2. Locked down

We will protect and secure any data you share with us. Your account is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You must prevent unauthorized access to your account and Personal Information by selecting and protecting your password appropriately.

3. Straight talking

We will always talk your language and be open – no nonsense, no surprises.

4. You're in control

You decide if you want to hear from us and how you hear from us. You are free to unsubscribe from our Newsletter and if you want your details removed from our records you can simply contact us HERE

.

5. Just the essentials

No unnecessary information – if we do not need it, we won't collect it. The essentials include things like your full name, email address, phone number, payment details (including billing and credit card details should you purchase a poster), plus any other information you provide when communicating with us.

Protecting Your Privacy

At Poster Palace, we are 100% committed to protecting your privacy and security. We are customers ourselves of Poster Palace, so we totally appreciate and respect how important privacy is. For all Poster Palace services, the data controller — the company that is responsible for protecting your privacy— is Poster Palace.
