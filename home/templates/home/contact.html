Get In Touch

Have a question about your order? Need help with buying a product or logging in? Whatever your question we are here to help. You can check out our frequently asked questions (FAQ) below or complete the contact form and we will be in touch within two working days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does delivery cost?

Orders over £40.00 are free

Deliveries within the UK and Ireland are charged at 10% of your total order, so if your order is £30.00 delivery will be £3

Deliveries for the rest of the world are charged at 10% of your total order, so if your order is £30.00 delivery will be £3.

How quickly will I receive my order?

Deliveries within the UK and Ireland:

Most orders will be delivered within 3 working days, excluding weekends, as they are sent first class. Once your order has been despatched you will receive an email notification with the tracking number. If you have provided a mobile number you will receive a text message on the day giving you a delivery time slot of either 8am-2pm or 2pm-7pm.

If you have opted to deliver your order to a country other than the UK and Ireland, the delivery times will vary but you will still receive an email notification with the tracking number once your order has been despatched.

UK – within 3 working days

Europe – within 5 working days

USA & Canada – within 7-10 working days

Rest of World – within 12 working days

If you haven't received your order within the above timeframe complete the contact form above and we will look into it for you. To ensure we find your order quickly please provide the order number and date of order.

My product is damaged, what should I do?
We pride ourselves on looking after our customers and never want you to have to tolerate a product that isn't as good as it could be. Please take a photo and/or video using your mobile phone and send it in to us via email at posters@posterpalace.com. If the damage has been caused by us and/or our couriers we will of course replace it immediately at no extra charge.
